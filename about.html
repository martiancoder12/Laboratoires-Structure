About

Laboratoires Structure is a consulting and product design firm based in Montréal, helping organizations apply AI, optimize operations, and prepare for the next era of work.

We partner with teams who are building or transforming systems — from internal processes and SOPs to client-facing platforms and intelligent automation.

Our difference? We don't just apply AI tools — we design the operating logic behind them. Our work blends systems thinking, product strategy, and minimal software architecture to build solutions that are efficient, scalable, and deeply aligned with your business model.

Core Engagements

  • AI enablement & automation workshops
  • End-to-end process and SOP redesign
  • Strategic product planning & prioritization
  • Data and compliance transformation
  • Web and systems architecture consulting

We've worked with creative agencies, healthcare innovators, retail operators, and software startups to modernize how they think and operate.

Behind the scenes, we're also developing StructureOS — a modular AI-native productivity system built around natural language, automation, and real-world workflows. Every engagement helps shape this vision.
